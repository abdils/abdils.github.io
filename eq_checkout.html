Equipment Checkout App

This application was designed to be able to organize all of the video and camera equipment for the Digital Corps. Users will be able to check out cameras, lenses, backdrops, booms, etc. This will make sure all employees will be held accountable for what they check out, and it allows other employees to see what has already been checked out, and when it should be returned.
