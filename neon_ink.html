Neon Ink Blots

This project was inspired by the Rorschach Ink Blot studies conducted in the 1960's. The studies were psychological tests in which subjects' perceptions of inkblots were recorded and then analyzed using psychologicalinterpretation, complex algorithms, or both. I decided to design a shape or "blot" that resembembled a tree and also a mushroom cloud from an explosion. Both shapes have similar phyiscal features, but have completely seperate physciological relationships.
