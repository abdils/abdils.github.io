Alex Dils

About me

Name is Alex, friends call me Dils. A midwesterner at heart, I was born and raised in Indiana but have a global mentality. I'm a designer, an artist, thrill seeker, glass blower, pizza eater, coffee drinker, and nature lover. Lately I've been spending most of my creative time designing interfaces and animations for apps and websites, exploring photography, and making strange things in the glass studio.

Art

As an artist I am generating ideas, refining concepts, and producing work in a variety of mediums. 2-D (sketching, illustration, painting) 3-D (metal, glass found object) and 4-D (moving images)

Design

I strive to keep my concepts unique and my aesthetic clean . I focus on making visual assets for the web, application interfaces, and traditional print. A growing interest of mine has been designing keyframe-based animations to bring content to life.

3-D

Maintaining a high level of craft has always been a priority in my work, details are important. I like to get my hands dirty and work with multiple materials such as metals, glass, wood, and found objects to create unique hand-crafted look.
