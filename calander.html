Calander Design Project

The Amalgamation is an interactive calander. This calander is designed for users to tear out perforated tickets that correlate with each day of the year. If the user has a particuallary memorable day, then he/she would write a quick note about the event. Then the ticket is placed inside the build-a-box that can be assembled from the cover of the calander book, and is assembled at the beggining of the year. At the conclusion of the year the user will be able to reflect on the contents inside the box.
